Travel Essentials Checklist

1. Documents & Money

  8. Emergency contact info
  9. Copies (physical & digital) of important documents

2. Clothing

  1. Weather-appropriate outfits
  2. Undergarments and socks
  3. Sleepwear
  4. Comfortable walking shoes
  5. Flip-flops / sandals
  6. Swimwear
  7. Jacket / raincoat / umbrella

3. Toiletries

  1. Toothbrush, toothpaste, floss
  2. Shampoo, conditioner, soap/body wash
  3. Face wash & moisturizer
  4. Sunscreen & lip balm with SPF
  5. Deodorant
  6. Hairbrush / comb
  7. Razor & shaving supplies
  8. Feminine hygiene products
  9. Small towel or travel towel

4. Health & Safety

  1. Personal medications (with prescriptions)
  2. First aid kit
  3. Masks & hand sanitizer
  4. Insect repellent
  5. Water bottle
  6. Eyeglasses / contact lenses
  7. Travel sickness pills

5. Tech & Electronics

  1. Phone + charger
  2. Power bank / portable charger
  3. Headphones / earphones
  4. Universal power adapter
  5. Camera + memory cards
  6. E-reader / Kindle (optional)
  7. Laptop / tablet (if needed)

6. Other Essentials

  1. Backpack / daypack
  2. Packing cubes / organizers
  3. Ziplock bags / plastic bags
  4. Snacks (energy bars, dry fruits)
  5. Travel pillow
  6. Notebook + pen
  7. Travel guidebook or offline maps
  8. Laundry bag

Pro Travel Tips

  • Always check weather before packing.
  • Roll clothes to save space.
  • Keep valuables in your carry-on.
  • Back up documents online (Google Drive, email, etc.).
